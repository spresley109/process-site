Initial Client Research

Augusta Care Pregnancy Center is located in Augusta, Georgia. This organization primarily operates in the Crisis Center business / industry within the Social Services sector. This organization has been operating for approximately 14 years. Augusta Care Pregnancy Center is estimated to generate $284,168 in annual revenues, and employs approximately 8 people at this single location.

During my research, I did not find a lot of information about the Augusta Care Preganacy Center outside of their website. I provided several photographs of the website, as well as, a few images of resources I found beyond their site.

The home page is quite cluttered and overwhelming to look at and navigate through. I do not feel like the purpose of their organization is conveyed clearly - you definitely have to do a little digging to figure it out. The home page mainly consists of an outdated flyer from an event (in repetiton down the page) with a lot of text.

I navigated to the donation page, and yet again, I feel like you have to have prior knowledge of the organization to understand and the donations page does not provide any more clarity. The donations page gives several options and a lot of information to fill out that may try the users patience.

There is a page for classes offered. I feel like this is more directed at the intended client. The CPC offers a Post Abortion support group. Parenting classes for pregnant/new mothers (as well as the fathers). Healthy relationship classes (an 8 week course). As well as, a Bible study offered every Thursday. Attendance in any of these classes gives mothers points that can be used at the Mommy Store.

I think that the information provided on the site is very important and much of it is necessary but it is displayed in an unnnecessarily complicated way. I found some information outside of their site that is a little more direct, including a Q and A about their services, etc. I found some information about the CPC on an affiliated church blog as well.
