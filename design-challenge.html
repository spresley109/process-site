Design Challenge

  • What is the problem you are trying to solve? To support women with unplanned pregnancies in the Augusta area.
  • Frame it as a design question. How does the Augusta Care Pregnancy website contribute to helping women in the Augusta area?
  • What is the desired ultimate impact? Increase outreach and donations to support these women.Develop a useful and effetive online presence for the Augusta Care Pregnancy Center.
  • What are some possible solutions to the problem? Increase social media presence (Facebook, Instagram, etc), include testimonies, make the website more scannable, show clear goals and hierarchy on homepage, and overall create a more clear/concise design that is not overwhelming.
  • What are some of the context and constraints? Small organization with little online presence. It is a little hazy knowing what your donations are actually going towards unless you have been directly involved.
  • Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again. How can I create a more inviting and clear online presence for Augusta CPC to help support women in a crisis?
